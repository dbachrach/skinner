You are invited to participate in this experiment on learning and memory, conducted by the Bjork Human Memory and Forgetting Lab at the University of California, Los Angeles. It will take approximately 60 minutes of your time. There are no anticipated risks or discomforts, and we hope that the results of the survey may contribute to a better understanding of how people learn. Participation in this study is voluntary. No identifying information will be linked to your performance in this study (your responses are anonymous). If you have any questions, comments or concerns about the research, please contact us at emsparck@ucla.edu. If you wish to ask questions about your rights as a research participant or if you wish to voice any problems or concerns you may have about the study to someone other than the researchers, please call the Office of the Human Research Protection Program at (310) 825-7122 or write to Office of the Human Research Protection Program, UCLA, 11000 Kinross Avenue, Suite 102, Box 951694, Los Angeles, CA 90095-1694.
